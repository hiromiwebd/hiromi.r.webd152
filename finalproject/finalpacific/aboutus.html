Who We Are?

In 2023, we celebrate our fifth year of opening resorts in the United States. We originally operated only in Japan, but chose California, a place we love, to take on the challenge of overseas business. We are celebrating 101 years since we opened our first hotel in Japan. In a little over 100 years, tourism in Japan and the hotel industry around the world have changed dramatically, but the next 100 years will be an era of even greater change. We are currently developing Western-style resorts, but we are also looking to open Japanese-style resorts in the future.
